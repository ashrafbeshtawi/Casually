Stop overcomplicating your life. Casually keeps you organized without the overwhelm.

Most productivity apps feel like a second job. They drown you in features, notifications, and guilt. Casually is different. It's task management that respects your time, your attention, and your pace. No pressure. No clutter. Just a clean space to think, plan, and get things done — on your own terms.

PROJECTS THAT MAKE SENSE
Organize your life into three natural categories:

- Projects: Big goals with multiple steps. Group tasks under projects, track progress, and watch them move from Active to Done.
- One-Off Tasks: Quick things that don't belong anywhere. Buy groceries. Call the dentist. Done.
- Routines: The stuff you do regularly. Morning workout. Weekly review. Keep showing up.

Every task flows through smart states — Active, Waiting, Blocked, and Done. You always know what needs your attention right now and what can wait. No more staring at an endless flat list wondering where to start.

PRIORITY SYSTEM THAT HELPS
Five color-coded priority levels from Highest (red) to Lowest (green) let you spot what matters at a glance. No reading required — just look at the colors. Set dependencies between tasks so blocked items stay out of your way until they're ready.

CHALLENGES — YOUR PERSONAL STREAK TRACKER
This is where Casually gets fun. Trying to quit a bad habit? Building a new one? Create a Challenge and watch the live timer climb — days, hours, minutes, seconds.

Every streak earns you a league rank:

    Seedling > Bronze > Silver > Gold > Platinum > Diamond > Master > Legend

Start at Seedling on day zero. Hit 3 days and earn Bronze. Push through a week for Silver. Make it to 14 days for Gold. The ranks keep climbing — 30 days for Platinum, 60 for Diamond, 120 for Master. Hit a full year and you become a Legend.

Slip up? No shame. Hit the relapse button, reset your timer, and start climbing again. The point isn't perfection — it's showing up. Every restart is proof you haven't given up.

HOME SCREEN WIDGET
Your active tasks, right on your home screen. No need to open the app to check what's next.

The widget shows all your active projects with their tasks, color-coded by priority. Tap a project to collapse or expand it — instant, no lag. See progress counts at a glance.

Need to add something quick? The "+" button opens task creation with a project picker. Capture the thought before it's gone. Looks great in both light and dark mode.

ACHIEVEMENTS WALL
Every task you complete lands in your Achievements — your personal trophy case. Scroll through everything you've accomplished, grouped by project.

On days when it feels like you're not making progress, open Achievements and see the proof. You've done more than you think.

EMOJI EVERYTHING
Make your tasks personal. Add emojis to projects, tasks, and challenges. Your task list should look like yours — not like a corporate spreadsheet.
